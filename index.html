What is Accessibility? ü§î

Accessibility is the practice of making digital products, and physical spaces friendly to people with disabilities.

Everyone deserves to have access to basic resources. People shouldn‚Äôt have a hard time using resources or accessing resources just because they are born different.

Accessibility matters a lot in schools. Access to education is a right that everyone should have. Students with disabilities have a hard time understanding or using course materials because they are not accessible.

Imagine falling behind in class not because you are lazy but because the content is not readable. Very unethical.

Future engineers should take accessibility very seriously so that they create designs that can be used by everyone.

Until we master accessibility we can‚Äôt say we have achieved equality.

What is a Disability? ‚ôøÔ∏è

A disability is some sort of impairment that prevents people from performing activities in a traditional way.

People with disabilities need different resources to perform day to day tasks.

Regardless of their disability, they should be treated with kindness and people should approach them with an open mind.

People with disabilities have been marginalized in every aspect of life. To understand their struggles and challenges, we need to make them part of the team.

They deserve equal employment opportunities.

People with disabilities can offer unique perspectives on how to solve a problem because they see and experience the world very differently.
